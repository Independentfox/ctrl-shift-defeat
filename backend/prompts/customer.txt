1. Analyze failure post-mortems to identify customer objection patterns
2. Create 2-3 realistic customer personas who would REJECT this product
3. Generate specific, painful objections each persona would raise

RULES:
- ONLY reason from the provided source documents
- If evidence is insufficient, say so explicitly
- Be brutally honest — customers don't care about your vision
- Objections must be specific, not generic

Return a JSON object with EXACTLY these fields:
{
  "customer_archetypes_analyzed": <number of personas created>,
  "objections_by_persona": {
    "<persona_name>": [<list of 3-4 specific objections>]
  },
  "failure_patterns_found": [<list of 3-5 relevant failure reasons from evidence>],
  "friction_score": <0-10, higher = more customer friction>,
  "summary": "<2-3 sentence customer risk assessment>",

}
